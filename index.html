Mr. Ashok Dahiphale

Principal

I am deeply honored to serve as the custodian of Swatantrya Sainik Raykumar B Gujar Secondary and Higher Secondary Prashala, entrusted with upholding its remarkable standards and guiding it towards a brighter future. This role is both a privilege and a responsibility that I embrace with great pride.

At our institution, we are dedicated to cultivating a passion for learning, nurturing creativity, and empowering our students to face the challenges of a rapidly evolving world. We celebrate their strengths, acknowledge their imperfections, and respect their individuality, allowing each student to grow at their own pace. Our committed faculty and staff work diligently to create an inclusive, supportive environment where every student—no matter how unique—can thrive. We believe that it’s often the misfits and oddballs who change the world, and we embrace the notion that it’s cool to be different.

Our holistic approach to education goes beyond academic achievement; it emphasizes character development and community spirit. Sports play a vital role in our school, and we take immense pride in the accomplishments of both our girls’ and boys’ teams, who have competed at the national level.

As the Principal, it is with great humility that I continue a legacy of exceptional leadership, knowing that leadership is not defined by title or gender, but by mindset and dedication to one’s work. This centennial celebration stands as a tribute to the unwavering commitment of generations, past and present, who have contributed to our school’s rich heritage.

This centennial celebration is a testament to the dedication of generations, past and present, who have contributed to the rich legacy of our school.

Together, with my dedicated team, I pledge to continue striving for excellence, ensuring that Swatantrya Sainik Raykumar B Gujar Secondary and Higher Secondary Prashala reaches even greater heights in the years to come.

Education

Education is the spark that ignites a child’s future. Our school exemplifies the cultural diversity that India is renowned for, merging age-old values with modern trends. Our teachers embody this blend, offering the perfect combination of tradition and innovation in their approach to education.

Student Development

At our school, we nurture well-rounded individuals by fostering personal growth and leadership through various initiatives. Our programs promote teamwork, resilience, and social responsibility, ensuring students develop both knowledge and character, preparing them to be future leaders in a global society.

Sports

At our institution, we believe in sports' power. Our campus features excellent facilities, including three football fields, a tennis court, volleyball courts, badminton courts, a basketball court, a 400-meter track, a cricket ground, a gym, and a swimming pool. These resources help students cultivate fitness alongside academics.

Activities

Learning goes beyond textbooks and classrooms. Our diverse school activities make education engaging and enjoyable. Students can choose from various extracurricular options, including guitar, piano, dance, and more, allowing them to explore their passions and discover new talents.

Swatantrya Sainik Raykumar B Gujar Secondary and Higher Secondary Prashala

Swatantrya Sainik Raykumar B Gujar Secondary and Higher Secondary Prashala was established in 1999 to cater to the educational needs of the village of Talegaon Dhamdhere and its vicinity. Since then, it has been a beacon of education in our community, offering quality schooling for both school and junior college students. Our institution is dedicated to nurturing young minds, with a special focus on instilling strong values, academic excellence, and holistic development.

Despite limited resources, our school is committed to providing a well-rounded education, empowering students to succeed in their academic and personal lives. We offer diverse programs in Arts, Science, and Commerce for junior college students, equipping them with the knowledge and skills they need to pursue higher education and meaningful careers.

With a dedicated team of teachers, we foster a supportive learning environment that emphasizes discipline, creativity, and leadership. Our students participate in a variety of activities, including sports, cultural events, and academic competitions, ensuring their all-round development.

Swatantrya Sainik Raykumar B Gujar Secondary and Higher Secondary Prashala continues to serve as a pillar of education in Talegaon Dhamdhere, shaping the future of our students and helping them achieve their fullest potential.

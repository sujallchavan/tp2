The Ethos
Serving educational requirement since 1925, Barnes welcomes families from all nationalities and offers the very best in Indian Education. This is within the context to an age-old boarding community, and is epitomized by the school's philosophy of 'the best for every child'.
Our Mission
To facilitate and support learning, in a secure, secular environment, that develops the students' intellectual, moral and creative capabilities promoting awareness, understanding and tolerance; thereby enabling them to harmoniously resolve dynamic challenges of a diverse global community.
Our Vision
We seek to forge a lifelong bond with our students, empowering them to represent our core values of excellence, humility, and inclusiveness while constructively contributing to the community as leaders, civil servants and achievers in sporting or other chosen fields.

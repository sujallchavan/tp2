The Pre-Primary Years

The initial years are the most significant ones in shaping a child's physical, emotional, cognitive, and social development. Our curriculum and activities for the Pre-Primary section ensure all these aspects are addressed.

Play School

Timings: 9:00 am to 12:00 noon. Age Criteria: 2.5 to 3.5 years.

It has a separate indoor play area fully equipped with toys, learning aids, and apparatus to help the little children develop their motor skills. Daily activities are conducted with the aim of enhancing their finger movements and improving hand-eye coordination.

Nursery

Timings: 9:00 am to 12:00 noon. Age Criteria: 3.5 to 4.5 years.

The curriculum is designed to channel a child's natural inquisitiveness to question, discover, and explore the world around them.
